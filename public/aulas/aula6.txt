早 = zao3 = early / good morning (informal)
您早！= nin2 zao3 = good morning!
您身体好吗？= nin2 shen1 ti3 hao3 ma = how is your body (health)?
你的爸爸的身体 = ni3 de ba4 ba de shen1 ti3 = your father’s health
还 = hai2 = still, yet (adv)
我还没有吃。= wo3 hai2 mei2 you3 chi1 = i still not yet eat.
** 还没有！= hai2 mei2 you3 = Not yet!
** 今天 = jin1 tian1 = today
你今天有空吗？= ni3 jin1 tian1 you3 kong4 ma = are you free today?
天 = tian1 = sky , God, day
我的天啊！= wo3 de tian1 a = My God!!!
大哥哥 = da4 ge1 ge = big older brother
小弟弟 = xiao3 di4 di = small younger brother
奶妈 = nai3 ma1 = nanny （old society）
谢谢你！= xie4 xie ni3 = thank you!
不客气！= bu2 ke4 qi4 = you are welcome (no need to be polite)
气 = qi4 = air
天气 = tian1 qi4 = weather
今天的天气好吗？= jin1 tian1 de tian1 qi4 hao3 ma = how is today’s weather?
客人 = ke4 ren2 = guest
他是我的客人。= ta1 shi4 wo3 de ke4 ren2 = he is my guest.
客气 = Ke4 qi4 = polite
她很客气。= ta1 hen3 ke4 qi4 = she is very polite.
