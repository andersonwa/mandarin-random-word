不 = bu4 / bu2 = no, not (before the verb and adj) to negate
我不是女人 = wo3 bu2 shi4 nv3 ren2 = i am not woman.
我不好 = wo3 bu4 hao3 = i am not well.
我不好吗？= wo3 bu4 hao3 ma = am i not good?
吃 = chi1 = to eat
你吃吗？= ni3 chi1 ma = do you eat?
我不吃。= wo3 bu4 chi1 = i do not eat.
知道 = zhi1 dao4 = to know
我不知道。= wo3 bu4 zhi1 dao4 = i do not know.
家 = jia1 = family
你家人好吗？= ni3 jia1 ren2 hao3 ma = how are your family members?
您 = nin2 = you (polite tone)
心 = xin1 = heart
她 = ta1 = she
他 = ta1 = he
它 = ta1 = it
您好！= nin2 hao3 = hello (polite)
您好吗？= nin2 hao3 ma = how are you (polite)?
